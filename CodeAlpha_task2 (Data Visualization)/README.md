# E-commerce Dashboard

### Project Overview
This interactive Tableau Public dashboard transforms raw transactional sales data into strategic, actionable business insights. Designed with an excutive audience in mind, the dashboard tracks revenue velocity, identifies seasonal purchasing patterns, and breaks down performance across distinct categories.

### Key Business Insights
1. Executive KPI Metrics
- Total Revenue: Tracks overall financial footprint and gross scale.
- Total Units Sold: Measures physical operational throughput and logistical deman
- Average Order Value (AOV): Calculates the average dollar amount captured per transaction, serving as the baseline efficiency metrics.
2. Regional Performance Heatmap
It instantly highlights high-revenue zones and maps regional customer preferences by tier.
3. Monthly Seasonality Trends
A continuous time-series line chart tracking monthly revenue fluctuations. It visually exposes customer behaviors, such as how New buyers spike during holiday promotional cycles,while premium buyers maintain a stable baseline.
